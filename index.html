Who I Am

About Me

Electrical Engineer with field expertise in solar PV operations and a strong foundation in embedded systems, IoT, and technical education.

I am an Electrical Engineer from UET Lahore (CGPA 3.54) currently working as an O&M Engineer at Nizam Energy Pvt. Ltd., where I operate and maintain a 3.2 MW grid-connected solar PV plant featuring single-axis sun-tracking systems in Gujranwala, Pakistan.

Alongside my O&M role, I am actively supervising the installation and commissioning of an additional 1 MW solar plant — coordinating civil and electrical contractors, managing vendors, and overseeing equipment testing and pre-commissioning activities.

My background spans both field engineering and academia — I previously served as an Electrical Technology Instructor at GCT Gujranwala. I am also deeply interested in embedded systems, IoT, and robotics, with multiple engineering projects developed during my university years.

I was competitively selected as a PEEF Scholar and served as IEEE Student Branch Technical Head at UET Lahore, Narowal Campus.
